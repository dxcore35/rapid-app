FAQ

I changed devices. Will my purchase be lost?

No. Rapid is a one-time purchase with no subscription. Use the same Apple ID used for purchase.

What inputs are supported in v1.0?

PDF import, web links (URL), plain text, and clipboard text.

How do I restore my purchase?

Install Rapid on your new device with the same Apple ID used for purchase, then redownload the app from the App Store.

Do I need an account to use Rapid?

No. Rapid does not require account creation to use core features.

Why can some web links fail to import?

Some websites block extraction or require login/paywall access. If parsing fails, try another public article URL.

What should I send when I report a bug?

Include: device model, iOS version, app version, exact steps to reproduce, and screenshots or screen recording.
